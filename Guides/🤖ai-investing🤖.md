# AI In Investing

Here are some prompts to use when you're researching companies.


1. *What are the different components of `[industry]`? Use `[public company names]` in your examples.*

    - With this prompt, you can get an overview of the different companies in the industry you've specified and which ones can be considered direct competitors to one another.

2. *What is `[company name's]` economic moat? Do not include subjective concepts like "expertise" or "skilled workforce"*.

3. *I want you to act as an investment manager who has a fiduciary duty to advise clients impartially. Knowing this, outline a potential bear thesis for `[company name or company ticker symbol]`*


## `Use An AI Tool To Read The Reports...`


A tool called *`https://askyourpdf.com`* allows you to upload a PDF that you can ask simple Yes/No questions. Good questions include something like:

1. *Are there any legal proceedings against `[company name]`?*
2. *How does `[company name]` protect its intellectual property?*
3. *Who are `[company name's]` main competitors?*
4. *Is the `[company name's]` founder still actively involved with the company?*
5. *Has `[company name]` experienced any weakness within its financial reporting?*
6. *Is `[company name]` planning to do any share buybacks or issue a dividend?*
7. *What was `[company name's]` revenue/earnings growth rate last year?*
8. *Has `[company name's]` made any significant acquisitions in the last year?*
9. *Does `[company name]` have more cash or debt?*
10. *What is the breakdown of `[company name's]` revenue?*
11. *What sources of revenue does `[company name]` have?*
12. *Has `[company name]` issued more shares in the last year?*
