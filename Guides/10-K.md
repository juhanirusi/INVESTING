# What To Look For In The 10-K Report?

1. Intangibles

    - Patents, which are useless without a product(s).

2. Competition

    - Companies must list prospective competitors.

3. Lawsuits

    - Active & pending.

4. True economics of their business model

    - Especially useful for companies which are not yet profitable.


## Risks

- Generic

    - FOREX
    - Taxation
    - Seasonality
    - Competitors
    - "If all our customers disappear we won't have a business"

- Company Specific

    - Dependence on a single figure (Tesla)
    - Dependence on 1-2 suppliers/clients
    - Impact of COVID (or alike pandemics)
